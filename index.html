Game Instructions

Welcome to Guess the Avenger!
The instructions for this game are based on the traditonal Guess Who game. Start by entering your player name.Then say hi to check and see if another player is in the chat room. When you're both ready to begin you can both hit "New Game". This will generate a character for you. Take turns asking the other player yes or no questions about their character card. You can elimante options in the list of pictures on the right by clicking on a picture. Use the remaining character options to help you make a guess on one of your turns! If the other player guesses right before you hit the "You Guessed Right" button to let them know they won! To start another round simply hit the "New Game" button again
